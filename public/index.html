🗑️ Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your Lifeline account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

⚠️

Important Information

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted:

  • All memories and entries
  • Photos, videos, and audio recordings
  • Tags, emotions, and metadata
  • AI analysis data
  • Account settings and preferences

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the Lifeline app
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Scroll down to "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion by entering your password

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

founder@theplacewelive.org

Please include your account email address in the request.

Processing Time

• In-app deletion: Immediate
• Email request: Within 7 business days

💡

Before You Delete

Consider exporting your data first (Premium feature). This allows you to keep a backup of your memories even after deletion.
