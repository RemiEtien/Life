Lifeline Account Deletion Instructions

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, including your memories, photos, videos, and audio notes, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Lifeline app on your device.

  2. Navigate to the "Profile & Settings" section (the silhouette icon in the bottom-right corner of the main screen).

  3. In the settings menu, find and select the "Account Management" section.

  4. Tap the "Delete Account" button. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your decision.

Please note that account deletion is irreversible. All your data will be permanently erased from our servers. You will not be able to recover your memories once this process is complete.
